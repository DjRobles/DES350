The big 10

1.Good Design should reach its intended goal.

2.Good Design should have a theme or idea.

3. Good Design needs live after death.

4. Good Design is eye catching.

5. Good Design births new ideas.

6. Good Design is always changing.

7. Good Design is created with passion.

8. Good Design is made the best it can be.

9. Good Design has a solid yet simple color scheme.

10.Good Design is good design.
